Foly (Bronze Featherback)
Family: Notopteridae

Scientifc name: Notopterus notopterus

 

Available sizes: 200 - 300 g, 300 - 500 g

Types: Whole fish, Block meat

 

Pholi is a freshwater fish found in South and Southeast Asia. It is very tasty and has high nutrition value

 

Health benefits:

It is a good source of Protein.
