Cuttlefish
Family: Cephalopoda

Scientific name: Sepiida

 

Cuttlefish, related to the octopus and squid and characterized by a thick internal calcified shell called the cuttlebone.

 

Health benefits

This food is very low in Saturated Fat. It is also a good source of Vitamin C and B12, Calcium, Potassium and Zinc, Protein, Riboflavin, Iron, Phosphorus, Copper and Selenium.
