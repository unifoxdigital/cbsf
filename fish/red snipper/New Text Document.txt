Family: Lutjanidae

Scientifc name: Lutjanus campechanus

 

Red snapper is a lean, moist fish with a firm texture and a distinctive sweet, nutty flavor which makes it versatile for many flavor components.

 

Health benefits

Red snapper contains moderate levels of Omega-3 fatty acids, as well as significant amounts of Vitamin D and E, Selenium, Magensium and  Potassium.
