Basa (Catfish)
Family: Pangasiidae

Scientific name: Pangasius 

 

Available sizes: 1, 2, 3 Kg Up

Types: Gutted, Fillet, Butterfly Cut

 

Basa (Pangasius) is a species of catfish. These fish are important food fish with an international market. 

 

Health benefits

Basa are rich in protein and Omega-3 fatty acids and low in Calories and Sodium. Basa fillets contain no carbohydrate.
