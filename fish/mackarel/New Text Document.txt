Family: Scombridae

Scientific name: Scomberomorus commerson, Scomberomorus guttatus, Rastrelliger kanagurta

 

Mackerel is an important food fish that is consumed worldwide. It is found in temperate and tropical seas around the world.

 

Health benefits

Mackerel fish is a very nutrient-dense food and packs in tons of Protein, Omega-3 fatty acids, Vitamin B12,  Selenium, Niacin, and Phosphorus for a low amount of Calories.
